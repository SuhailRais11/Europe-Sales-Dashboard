# Europe-Sales-Dashboard
1- Description:
A visually interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing product sales, profit, and regional performance across different segments and countries.
This project presents a Product Sales Analysis Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI, designed to provide actionable insights into sales performance, profitability, and market distribution.

2- Purpose:
The main objective of this dashboard is to:
Monitor overall sales and profit performance
Analyze product-wise and segment-wise trends
Compare regional (country-level) contributions
Enable data-driven decision-making through interactive visuals

3- Key Features:
Sales Trend Analysis: Monthly sales performance visualization to identify growth patterns and seasonality
Product Performance: Comparison of total sales across different products
Segment Insights: Profit and sales distribution across business segments (Government, Small Business, Enterprise, etc.)
Geographical Analysis: Country-wise sales and profit breakdown
KPIs Overview:
Total Sales
Total Profit
Total Units Sold
Interactive Filters:
Country
Segment

4- Tools:
Microsoft Power BI
Data modeling and transformation using Power Query
DAX for calculated measures

5- Use cases:
Business performance tracking
Sales strategy optimization
Market and segment comparison
Executive-level reporting dashboards
